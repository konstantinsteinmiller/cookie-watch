Cat
•Make Cat a little bigger and lower him down a little more.
•Cat will be inactive on the first half of the run. He’ll be asleep.

Instruction Card
Let’s add a dedicated instruction screen that only appears in the first level when the player stands next to the cookie.

What should appear:

Two images side-by-side:

“Tap to break the cookie” (image: mouse next to cracking cookie)

“Swipe down to play dead” (image: mouse playing dead)

A small “Tap to continue” appears center-bottom of the instruction card.

A third image appears alone:
“Don’t let the Cat catch you.”

“Tap to continue”. Tapping exits the card and the player can begin breaking the cookie.

Player breaking the cookie should stir the Cat eventually and wake him up. This should practice the player’s down swipe
to play dead. The Cat will fall back asleep afterward. Player death can happen here if they don’t play dead in time.

Player returns to tapping at any time. Break the cookie and return back to the hole.

The Cat will wake up during their return home.

Engaging the Cookie
Avoid locking the player into position at the cookie. It feels a bit sticky here.
